## Your First 24 Hours In Crypto

Most people spend weeks "preparing" to start. You don't need weeks.

You need 24 hours — and a clear sequence. Do these five things, in this order, today.

:::checklist
### Step 1 — Create Your Wallet (20 min)
- Download MetaMask at metamask.io — browser extension or mobile app
- Create a new wallet and set a strong password
- **Write your 12-word seed phrase on paper** — no photos, no digital notes, no screenshots
- Store it somewhere physically secure — safe, lockbox, sealed envelope
- Lock MetaMask, re-open it, and test your password before moving on
:::

:::checklist
### Step 2 — Lock Down Your Exchange (10 min)
- Log in to Coinbase or Kraken
- Go to Settings → Security
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication using **Google Authenticator or Authy — not SMS**
- Store your 2FA backup codes somewhere offline and separate from your seed phrase
:::

:::checklist
### Step 3 — Make Your First Purchase (10 min)
- Buy $25–$100 of Bitcoin — enough to make it real, not enough to panic about
- Confirm the transaction completed in your exchange transaction history
- Do not move it anywhere yet — just let it sit
:::

:::checklist
### Step 4 — Set Your Recurring Buy (5 min)
- Find "Recurring Buy" in Coinbase or "Recurring Order" in Kraken
- Set $25–$50 weekly or monthly — an amount you genuinely will not miss
- Choose Bitcoin, or split 70% BTC / 30% ETH
- Turn it on and do not adjust it for at least 30 days
:::

:::checklist
### Step 5 — Send A Test Transaction To Your Wallet (15 min)
- Open MetaMask and copy your wallet address (starts with 0x... for Ethereum)
- **Triple-check the address** — one wrong character means permanent loss
- Send $5–$10 from your exchange to MetaMask
- Wait for confirmation — usually 10–30 minutes
- Verify the amount arrived in MetaMask before sending anything larger
:::

:::action
You've done it. Most people who say they want to get into crypto never complete these five steps. You just did. From this point forward, every week your recurring buy runs is a week you're building — whether the market is up or down.
:::
